What's Next App (work in progress)

View on GitHub

This andriod/IOS mobile app uses React Native Expo and Typescript to allow users to access the content of Dr. Lisa Baker's "What's Next" book. As part of the Samford software development course, teams are given a client to build a project for during the semester. Our client was Dr. Baker, and her mission has been to help parents of children with illnesses navigate the hospitalization process. This app takes Dr. Baker's helpful tips and information and presents it in an easy to click through form. My personal contributions to this project include UI design and some of the app functionality. While the app is not yet available for users, some members of our team are continuing to work on finalizing it. I am unfortunately not able to assist with this due to a busy final semester, but I would love to see the What's Next app in the App Store and Play Store.
